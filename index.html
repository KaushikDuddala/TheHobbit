Pricing

The pricing is absolutely none! However, at the end of your adventure (most likely including a pile of gold), you'll have to give up some of that. If you refuse, our compensation shall be a few organs!

Satisfaction

You'll most likely be very satisfied. We'll try our best to make sure you end up on a real adventure. However, if you decide you didn't like your adventure, we can give you a refund on your life!

Safety

We will ensure your safety to the best of our abilities, which is none! It's an adventure, have fun and no guarantees on your life!
